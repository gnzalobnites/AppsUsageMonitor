Monitor the time you spend on your apps with configurable awareness banners. Get detailed statistics, pie charts, and a dynamic theme that adapts to your usage time.

Main features:
* Configurable awareness banners (DEMO from 10 seconds)
* Intuitive interaction: 1 click expands, 2nd click closes
* Detailed statistics with pie charts per app
* Dynamic theme with colors based on usage time
* High precision using Android's UsageStatsManager
* Persistent dark/light theme
